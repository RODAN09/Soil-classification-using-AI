Why AI Over Traditional methods

Soil classification using AI is essential for improving agriculture, environmental conservation, and infrastructure development. In agriculture, AI-driven soil classification helps farmers understand soil quality and fertility, allowing them to choose the right crops and optimize fertilizer usage, leading to better yields and reduced costs. Environmental conservation also benefits from AI-based soil classification, as it helps monitor soil degradation, erosion, and land restoration efforts. By analyzing soil properties, AI can contribute to combating desertification and climate change.
